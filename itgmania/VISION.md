# Unified StepMania Vision

## The Ultimate Goal
To create the definitive, universal rhythm game platform that unifies the fragmented StepMania ecosystem. "Unified StepMania" aims to integrate the best features from all major forks (ITGMania, Etterna, OutFox, NotITG) and official franchises (DDR, ITG, PIU) into a single, cohesive, community-driven experience.

2.  **Economy & Progression:** A persistent layer of engagement featuring a virtual economy (Bobcoin), a marketplace for assets, and a fitness tracking career mode (Gym).
3.  **Modern Connectivity:** Seamless online multiplayer, real-time leaderboards (Tournament), and live spectator tools.
4.  **Legacy Compatibility:** Absolute backward compatibility with Simfiles, Themes, and Lua scripts from SM5.0/5.1.

## Architectural Principles
*   **Monorepo:** All dependencies and submodules managed in a single repository for consistency.
*   **Lua-First:** Empower creators by exposing deep engine hooks to Lua.
*   **Safety:** Maintain a secure sandbox while allowing creative freedom.

## Conclusion
Unified StepMania is not just a simulator; it is a platform for the next generation of rhythm gamers, combining physical fitness, competitive esports, and creative expression.
