## Unified Dashboard
Upon starting the game, you are greeted by the Unified Dashboard (unless configured to auto-launch Arcade). This hub provides access to all major game modes.

*   **Arcade:** The classic gameplay experience. Select songs, courses, and play for high scores.
*   **Gym:** Fitness-focused mode with calorie tracking, weight logging, and specialized workout playlists.
*   **Tournament:** Competitive ladder system. View your global ranking, ELO, and join upcoming matches.
*   **Economy:** Manage your Bobcoin wallet, mine coins, and purchase items from the Marketplace.
*   **Missions:** Objective-based "Quest Mode" where you complete specific challenges to unlock content.
*   **NetPlay:** Join online lobbies to play with others in real-time.

## Arcade Mode
Standard StepMania gameplay.
*   **Styles:** Single, Double, Versus, Routine, Couple.
*   **Modifiers:** Press `Start` after selecting a song to access the Options Menu.
    *   *New:* "Judge Scale" allows you to tighten timing windows (J1-J8) similar to Etterna.
    *   *New:* "Screen Filter" and "ProTiming" visual aids are available by default.

## Gym Mode
Designed for fitness enthusiasts.
*   **Dashboard:** Displays your current weight, BMI, daily calorie goal, and login streak.
*   **Workouts:** Select from pre-generated playlists (e.g., "Cardio Blast", "Leg Day") that target specific difficulty ranges and durations.
*   **Tracking:** Calories burned are saved to your profile history.

## Tournament Mode
The competitive backbone of Unified StepMania.
*   **Ladder:** View the top players ranked by ELO.
*   **Matches:** See a schedule of upcoming automated tournaments.
*   **Prizes:** Win Bobcoin for placing high in tournaments.

## Economy & Marketplace
A fully integrated virtual economy powered by Bobcoin.
*   **Marketplace:** Browse and purchase items such as:
    *   **Song Packs:** Unlock new music.
    *   **Themes/Skins:** Customize the look of the game.
    *   **Boosts:** Temporary XP or Coin multipliers.
*   **Wallet:** View your transaction history (Mining rewards, Purchases, Winnings).
*   **Mining:** The game simulates "mining" Bobcoin based on your gameplay performance. Better scores = Higher Hashrate.
*   **Betting:** Before competitive matches, you can wager coins on the outcome.

## Mission Mode
A dedicated mode for completing specific tasks.
*   **Objective Types:**
    *   *Clear X Songs*
    *   *Achieve Full Combo*
    *   *Burn X Calories*
    *   *Defeat Boss Songs*
*   **Rewards:** Completing missions grants XP and unique items (Avatar Frames, Titles).

## Online Play
Connect to the StepMania Online (SMO) network.
*   **Lobbies:** Chat with other players in room-based lobbies.
*   **Multiplayer:** Synchronized gameplay with real-time score comparison.

## Advanced Options
*   **Simply Love Integration:** The platform fully supports the "Simply Love" theme structure for ITG-faithful gameplay.
*   **Feature Parity:** Unified StepMania includes mechanics inspired by DDR (Freeze Arrows, Shock Arrows), ITG (Mines, Hands), and PIU (Co-op).
